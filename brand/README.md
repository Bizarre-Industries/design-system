# Brand

`identity.json` is the permanent identity contract: Bizarre Industries, the Gravity Well mark, Signal Lime (`#C6FF24`), the exact slogan `CATCH THE STARS`, approved typography roles, layout modes, and organization names. `assets.json` is the canonical asset inventory and records approval, media type, SHA-256, licensing, and derivative-to-master relationships.

The mark geometry is preserved in `logo/source/`. The clean mark-only SVG masters and selected variable-font masters live under `packages/assets/`; `packages/assets/generated/` and the published `@bizarre/assets` package are deterministic derivatives. Supplied PNGs are visual references, not editable or publishable masters. `examples/identity-proof/index.html` is derived offline evidence and is not an identity authority.

Precision Signal is the canonical expression; Editorial Monument and Workshop Stamp are controlled derivatives. The full Gravity Well mark is approved at 48 px and above. A simplified sub-48 px mark, mono variants, wordmarks and formal lockups, Arabic display pairing and translated voice, and a custom Helling identity remain deferred creative work.

# Brand

`identity.json` is the versioned identity contract: Bizarre Industries, the Gravity Well mark, Signal Lime (`#C6FF24`), the exact slogan `CATCH THE STARS` and its meaning “Make the distant tangible,” approved typography roles, layout modes, the single-identity model, and the native-integration policy. Only the company name and Gravity Well geometry are non-negotiable; the other values are current governed decisions that require an explicit versioned revision to change. `assets.json` is the approved, publishable asset inventory and records media type, SHA-256, licensing, and derivative-to-master relationships. `provisional-assets.json` is a separate nonpublishable working inventory. An asset may never appear in both manifests.

The mark geometry is preserved in `logo/source/`. The clean mark-only SVG masters and selected variable-font masters live under `packages/assets/`; `packages/assets/generated/` and the published `@bizarre/assets` package are deterministic derivatives. The owner-selected left Continuous Lens Aperture v2 remains governed-provisional while its numerical geometry and production evidence are completed, and every current Atlas SVG that depends on it is isolated in `brand/provisional-assets.json`; those working files remain available below `packages/atlas/generated/` without entering the public asset package. Supplied PNGs are visual references, not editable or publishable masters. `examples/identity-proof/index.html` is derived offline evidence and is not an identity authority.

Precision Signal is the canonical expression; Editorial Monument and Workshop Stamp are controlled derivatives. The full Gravity Well mark is approved at 48 px and above in exactly two monochrome figure colors: Signal Lime or black. Mixed figure color, gradients, spectrum, or material effects on the mark are forbidden. A simplified sub-48 px mark, wordmarks and formal lockups, Arabic display pairing and translated voice, and platform-specific integration evidence remain deferred work. No product-specific Bizarre identity will be created.
